Tickets

Tickets will be available here. They'll cost $50 NZD. If you have trouble with the cost, let us know.

Tickets go on sale on Thursday 11th October. They'll be released in three waves, at the following times: 08:00 NZT, 16:00 NZT, 23:59 NZT.

We're releasing our tickets in waves like this because it looks like there's a chance that purplecon will sell out quickly. We are really keen on having a diverse audience, we want to try and make tickets available for people with different commitments, lifestyles, and from different locations. We are letting you know the date and time our tickets are available so that you can plan ahead if you need to.

We have a (very) small reserve of tickets that won't be in the general pool of tickets, these will be used for volunteers, speakers and stragglers. We're also planning on live-streaming and recording the talks so everyone can join in.

Merch

Just in time for the cherry blossom season, we have various wearable purplecon merch available for purchase! You can just buy it online here.

We're trying it out this way. We think that being able to order the shirts in your own time will save on queuing and handling of t-shirts on the day. If you'd like to wear your shirt to the con, then be sure to order it with plenty of time to spare!
